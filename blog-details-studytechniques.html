Study Techniques

admin October 9th, 2021 topic 1,

Studying and revision have always been a burden and a struggle for students. That is due to the wrong methods of studying. Empirical research has shown that our learning methods turn out to be a wasted effort. So in today’s blog, we will give you some effective advice that will help you to study cleverly Without wasting your efforts.

It’s great to repeat the affirmations that other people have created first, but it’s better to create your own based on what you want to manifest. You can take ideas from these affirmations:


1. Spaced repetition of key ideas: If learners return to their study of a topic from time to time, they will remember it better. Likewise, if they interleave the study of different topics, they will learn better and they will improve their retention and knowledge than if they had studied them one at the same time in sequence.


2. Embrace difficulties: Students haven’t discovered yet the benefit of making mistakes while studying, because while students make errors their learning will be improved and they will recall the information easier.


3. Increase Your Abilities: Students should have a growth mindset and the belief that learning and intelligence are malleable rather than fixed. A growth mindset will push students to focus on learning to be aimed at discovering new information and new skills, to improve their performance, and growing with increasing challenges.


4. Take notes from the textbook: this can be an effective study technique if you’re reading actively, so summarize the information and put it in your own words.


5. Do not cram a few weeks before the exam: do not leave things for the last minute. Studying in advance is better, so when you revise you know what to work on and what you have an understanding off. Trust me five minutes extra to study can go a long way.


6. Make a study schedule, so you do not procrastinate, trust me it will help you long term. Plan the schedule so that you are studying more than you are on your phone.


7. Always pay attention to lecturers or teachers. Take notes in class or lectures, it will help... trust me.


8. Put distractions away, if you do not feel comfortable leaving your phone somewhere, put it on Airplane mode. The phone can be a huge distraction by all the notifications, and notification lights.
